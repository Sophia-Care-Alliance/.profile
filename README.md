# Sophia Care Alliance

## Stewarding Human-Centered Operating Systems™ (HCOS™)

Sophia Care Alliance exists to help people and organizations build healthier systems.

We steward the development of **HCOS™ (Human-Centered Operating Systems™)**—a human-centered framework and AI-powered decision intelligence platform that helps organizations better understand complex systems, improve decision-making, reduce unnecessary human burden, and create the conditions where people and organizations can flourish.

## Our Mission

We believe:

- Healthy people create healthier systems.
- Healthier systems help people flourish.
- Technology should strengthen people—not replace them.
